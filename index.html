What We Bake

Fresh From Our Ovens
To Your Table

Every morning, our ovens fire up before sunrise to bring you the freshest baked goods in Buena Park. From artisan breads to custom cakes, everything is made with love and the finest ingredients.

Fresh Baked Bread

Sourdough, French baguettes, wheat, rye, and specialty loaves baked fresh every morning. Crusty on the outside, soft and warm inside. The way bread should be.

Donuts & Pastries

Glazed, cake, filled, and frosted. Our donuts are a Buena Park tradition. Plus Danish pastries, cinnamon rolls, and turnovers. Come early for the best selection.

Custom Cakes

Birthday, wedding, anniversary, or any celebration. Our custom cakes are designed to your vision and taste incredible. Order at least 48 hours in advance.

Cookies & Brownies

Chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, snickerdoodles, and fudgy brownies. Baked fresh daily in small batches. Perfect for snacking, gifting, or satisfying a sweet tooth.

Pies & Tarts

Apple, cherry, pecan, pumpkin, and seasonal fruit pies with flaky, buttery crusts. Made the old-fashioned way with real fruit and homemade fillings.

Breakfast Items

Muffins, scones, croissants, and breakfast sandwiches. Start your morning right with freshly baked goodness. Coffee available too. The best breakfast in Buena Park.

Sandwiches & Deli

Fresh sandwiches made on our own bread with premium deli meats and cheeses. Turkey club, Italian sub, and more. Lunch done right, bakery style.

Seasonal Specials

Holiday cookies, Valentine treats, Easter breads, and Thanksgiving pies. We celebrate every season with limited-time baked goods that make every occasion special.

About The Bakery

Buena Park's Neighborhood
Bakery For Over 20 Years

The Bakery has been a cornerstone of the Buena Park community for over two decades. What started as a small family operation has grown into the neighborhood's most beloved bakery, where generations of families start their mornings and celebrate their milestones.

Our philosophy is simple: use real ingredients, bake with care, and treat every customer like family. We still use the same recipes our founders perfected years ago, because when something works this well, you do not change it. You just keep making it fresh every single day.

From the first loaf of bread that comes out of our ovens at dawn to the last custom cake picked up before closing, everything we make carries the warmth and dedication that has made The Bakery a Buena Park institution.
